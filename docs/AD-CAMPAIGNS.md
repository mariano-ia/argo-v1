# Argo One — US Paid Acquisition Campaign Strategy

**Product:** Argo One (behavioral profiling for young athletes 8-16)
**Market:** United States
**Pricing:** $14.99 (1 profile) | $34.99 (3 profiles) | $49.99 (5 profiles)
**Goal:** Direct-response conversions (purchases)
**Date:** April 2026

---

## 1. Channel Strategy

### Primary Channels

| Channel | Budget % | Why | Expected CPA |
|---------|----------|-----|---------------|
| **Meta (Facebook + Instagram)** | 45% | Strongest parent targeting, visual storytelling, proven DTC conversions | $8-14 |
| **Google Ads (Search + PMax)** | 30% | High-intent capture, parents actively searching for youth sports tools | $10-18 |
| **TikTok** | 15% | Growing parent demographic, viral potential, low CPMs | $6-12 |
| **Pinterest** | 10% | "Planful parent" audience, long content shelf life, low competition | $7-13 |

### Why NOT other channels

- **YouTube (standalone):** Covered inside PMax and Meta video placements. No standalone budget until creative library is proven.
- **LinkedIn:** Wrong audience (B2B). Reserve for Argo SaaS (coaches/clubs) later.
- **Snapchat:** Teen-skewed. Parents are secondary on Snap.
- **X/Twitter:** Low conversion intent for parenting products. Poor targeting granularity.

### Channel Phasing

- **Weeks 1-3:** Meta + Google Search only (prove product-market fit)
- **Weeks 4-6:** Add TikTok (once winning creative is identified)
- **Weeks 7+:** Add Pinterest, scale winners

## 2. Audience Segments

### Segment 1: "The Competitive Sports Parent"

- **Demographics:** Parents 30-50, HHI $75K+, suburban
- **Interests:** Youth sports leagues, travel teams, USSSA, AAU basketball, club soccer, Little League
- **Behaviors:** Follows team scheduling apps (TeamSnap, GameChanger), buys sports equipment online, attends tournaments
- **Messaging angle:** "Give your athlete the edge coaches notice"
- **Custom audience:** Email list of parents who visited /pricing or /one but didn't purchase
- **Lookalike:** 1% LAL from purchasers; 2% LAL from site visitors who spent 60s+

### Segment 2: "The Development-Focused Parent"

- **Demographics:** Parents 28-45, college-educated, interest in child development
- **Interests:** Growth mindset, parenting podcasts (Good Inside, Raising Good Humans), child psychology, Montessori
- **Behaviors:** Reads parenting blogs, follows child development accounts, buys educational products
- **Messaging angle:** "Understand how your child thinks, competes, and connects"
- **Custom audience:** Visitors to /blog articles about archetypes or DISC
- **Lookalike:** 1% LAL from completed profiles (email match)

### Segment 3: "The Multi-Sport Family"

- **Demographics:** Parents 30-48, 2+ children, active family lifestyle
- **Interests:** Multiple youth sports, family fitness, outdoor activities, sports camps
- **Behaviors:** Registered for multiple sports leagues, purchases across sport categories
- **Messaging angle:** "3 profiles for $34.99 - understand what makes each of your kids tick"
- **Custom audience:** Purchasers of 1-profile pack (upsell to 3-pack)
- **Lookalike:** 1% LAL from 3-pack and 5-pack buyers

### Segment 4: "The Coach's Inner Circle"

- **Demographics:** Parents 28-50 who are also volunteer coaches or team managers
- **Interests:** Coaching certifications, Positive Coaching Alliance, youth coaching resources, NAYS
- **Behaviors:** Admins of youth sports Facebook groups, follows coaching accounts
- **Messaging angle:** "The same tool pro coaches use, now for your family"
- **Custom audience:** Website visitors from /play paths (came through a coach link, now retarget as parent buyer)
- **Lookalike:** 2% LAL from Argo SaaS tenant email list

### Segment 5: "The Season-Start Parent"

- **Demographics:** Parents 30-50, any income
- **Interests:** Sport-specific (targeted seasonally: soccer fall/spring, basketball winter, baseball spring/summer, swimming year-round)
- **Behaviors:** Seasonal sport registration, recently joined new team or league
- **Messaging angle:** "New season, new team? Know your kid's strengths before day one"
- **Custom audience:** Seasonal retargeting pools refreshed quarterly
- **Lookalike:** Broad 3-5% LAL for cold prospecting during season peaks

### Segment 6: "The Gift-Giver"

- **Demographics:** Grandparents 50-70, aunts/uncles 25-45
- **Interests:** Unique gifts for kids, sports gifts, experience gifts
- **Behaviors:** Shops online for children's gifts, follows "gift idea" content
- **Messaging angle:** "The most meaningful gift for a young athlete: understanding who they are"
- **Timing:** Heavy rotation Nov-Dec (holiday), May (Mother's/Father's Day angle for sports parents)

## 3. Ad Creative Concepts

### Creative 1: "The Locker Room Moment" (Video, 15-30s)

- **Hook:** "Your kid just lost the big game. What do you say?"
- **Body:** Quick cuts of common parent reactions (yelling from sidelines, awkward silence, forced positivity). Then pivot: "What if you knew exactly how your child processes competition?" Show Argo One odyssey on phone, then a parent reading the report with an "aha" expression.
- **CTA:** "Get their profile. $14.99. Takes 12 minutes."
- **Visual direction:** Warm, real-family feel. Not stock. Shot on iPhone aesthetic.
- **Format:** Vertical video (Reels/Stories/TikTok), 16:9 cut for Feed
- **Target segment:** Segment 1 (Competitive Sports Parent)

### Creative 2: "12 Archetypes" (Carousel, 5-7 cards)

- **Hook (Card 1):** "Which one is your kid?" with a grid of 4 archetype illustrations
- **Cards 2-5:** Individual archetype spotlights (The Captain, The Strategist, The Spark, The Guardian) with 1-line descriptions and recognizable kid behaviors
- **Card 6:** "12 archetypes. 36 variants. One 12-minute game."
- **Card 7 (CTA):** "Discover theirs for $14.99 - no account needed"
- **Visual direction:** Nautical-themed illustrations matching Argo brand. Each archetype has a distinct color and character.
- **Format:** Carousel (Meta Feed, Instagram)
- **Target segment:** Segment 2 (Development-Focused Parent)

### Creative 3: "The Ride Home" (Video, 30-45s)

- **Hook:** "The car ride home after practice says everything."
- **Body:** Split screen. Left: kid staring out window, quiet. Right: parent gripping steering wheel, unsure what to say. Text overlay builds the tension. Cut to: parent reading Argo report on phone. Key insight highlighted. Next scene: same car ride, but now parent asks the right question. Kid lights up.
- **CTA:** "Know what to say. Get their Argo profile."
- **Visual direction:** Cinematic but relatable. Golden hour lighting. Emotional resonance.
- **Format:** Vertical video (TikTok, Reels), landscape cut for YouTube/PMax
- **Target segment:** Segment 1 + Segment 2

### Creative 4: "Siblings Side-by-Side" (Static + Carousel)

- **Hook:** "Same parents. Same sport. Completely different athletes."
- **Body:** Two archetype cards side by side showing contrasting profiles (e.g., one kid is The Captain / Impulsor, the other is The Strategist / Estratega). "Understanding the difference changes everything."
- **CTA:** "Profile all your kids. 3 for $34.99."
- **Visual direction:** Clean, minimal. Two profile cards on a light background. Bold typography.
- **Format:** Static image (Feed), expandable to carousel showing more sibling combos
- **Target segment:** Segment 3 (Multi-Sport Family)

### Creative 5: "Coach Testimonial" (UGC-style Video, 20-30s)

- **Hook:** "I'm a youth soccer coach and I use this with every new player."
- **Body:** Coach (or actor in coach role) talking to camera about how understanding a kid's behavioral profile changes how they coach them. "But honestly? I wish I'd had this for my own kids first."
- **CTA:** "Try it yourself. No subscription. $14.99."
- **Visual direction:** UGC aesthetic. Filmed at a field or gym. Natural lighting, phone-quality.
- **Format:** Vertical video (TikTok, Reels, Stories)
- **Target segment:** Segment 4 (Coach's Inner Circle)

### Creative 6: "New Season Checklist" (Static/Story)

- **Hook:** "New season prep checklist:"
- **Body:** Checkbox list design: New cleats, Water bottle, Practice schedule, Know your kid's competitive profile. The last item is unchecked with an arrow pointing to the Argo logo.
- **CTA:** "12 minutes. $14.99. Zero guesswork."
- **Visual direction:** Clean checklist graphic on a sport-relevant background (grass, court, pool).
- **Format:** Story (Instagram/Facebook), static (Feed, Pinterest)
- **Target segment:** Segment 5 (Season-Start Parent)

### Creative 7: "Not a Personality Test" (Static/Video, 15s)

- **Hook:** "This is NOT a personality test for your kid."
- **Body:** "It's a behavioral profile built on the DISC model, designed specifically for young athletes ages 8-16. It doesn't label. It doesn't limit. It shows you how your child naturally competes, communicates, and connects." Show brief montage of the odyssey game UI.
- **CTA:** "See for yourself. $14.99. Results in your inbox."
- **Visual direction:** Bold text-on-screen format. Each line appears with emphasis. Argo brand colors.
- **Format:** Static (text-heavy for Facebook), short video (TikTok/Reels)
- **Target segment:** Segment 2 (Development-Focused Parent)

### Creative 8: "The Gift That Gets Them" (Video/Static)

- **Hook:** "Skip another jersey. Give them something that actually matters."
- **Body:** Quick montage of generic sports gifts (another trophy, another pair of shoes). Then: "Give them understanding." Show Argo One report being opened on a phone. Highlight a key insight. Parent smiles.
- **CTA:** "Argo One. From $14.99. The gift every sports parent wishes they'd found sooner."
- **Visual direction:** Warm, gift-giving context. Holiday or birthday setting.
- **Format:** Video 15-20s (all placements), static version for Pinterest
- **Target segment:** Segment 6 (Gift-Giver)

## 5. Funnel Architecture

### Cold Traffic (Top of Funnel)

```
Awareness Ad (Video/Carousel)
    |
    v
Landing Page (/one)
    |
    +--> Purchase (direct conversion, 2-4% expected)
    |
    +--> Exit (no purchase)
         |
         v
    Pixel fires: ViewContent event
```

### Warm Traffic (Middle of Funnel)

```
Retargeting Ad (7-day window)
    "Still thinking about it?"
    Different creative, same CTA
    |
    v
Landing Page (/one) — with urgency element
    |
    +--> Purchase (8-12% expected)
    |
    +--> Exit (again)
         |
         v
    Email capture attempt (exit intent or scroll-triggered)
    "Get a free sample archetype report"
```

### Hot Traffic (Bottom of Funnel)

```
Retargeting Ad (14-day window, excluders: purchasers)
    Testimonial creative or "Last chance" angle
    |
    v
Landing Page with social proof emphasis
    |
    +--> Purchase (12-18% expected)
```

### Retargeting Sequences

| Audience | Delay | Creative | Offer |
|----------|-------|---------|-------|
| Visited /one, no purchase | 1-3 days | "The Ride Home" video | Standard pricing |
| Visited /one, no purchase | 4-7 days | Testimonial/UGC | Standard pricing |
| Visited /one, no purchase | 8-14 days | "12 Archetypes" carousel + slight urgency | Consider limited-time bundle deal |
| Added to cart / started checkout | 1 day | "You're 1 step away" | Standard pricing |
| Added to cart / started checkout | 3-7 days | Social proof + reminder | Standard pricing |
| Purchased 1-pack | 7-30 days | "Profile your other kids" | 3-pack or 5-pack upsell |
| Blog readers (archetype content) | 1-7 days | "Not a Personality Test" ad | Standard pricing |

### Email Capture Strategy

- **Lead magnet:** "The 4 Athlete Personalities: A Parent's Quick Guide" (PDF)
  - 1-page visual guide to the 4 DISC axes in sports context
  - Gate behind email on blog or as exit-intent popup on /one
  - Follow-up sequence: 3 emails over 5 days
    1. Day 0: Deliver PDF + soft intro to Argo One
    2. Day 2: "Which one sounds like your kid?" (engagement email linking to archetype blog posts)
    3. Day 5: "See the full picture for $14.99" (direct CTA with testimonial)

- **Post-purchase email sequence:**
  1. Report delivery (immediate)
  2. Day 3: "How to use your child's profile" (tips email, builds trust)
  3. Day 7: "Got more than one athlete?" (upsell to 3-pack)
  4. Day 14: "Share Argo with a sports parent friend" (referral ask)

## 7. A/B Testing Plan

### Week 1-2: Creative Testing (Meta)

| Test | Variable A | Variable B | Success Metric | Min Sample |
|------|-----------|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| Hook type | Emotional ("The Ride Home") | Rational ("12 Archetypes") | CTR + CPA | 1,000 impressions each |
| Format | Video (15s) | Carousel (5 cards) | CTR + CPA | 1,000 impressions each |
| CTA text | "Get Their Profile" | "Discover Their Strengths" | CTR | 500 clicks each |

### Week 2-3: Audience Testing (Meta)

| Test | Variable A | Variable B | Success Metric | Min Sample |
|------|-----------|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| Segment | Competitive Sports Parent | Development-Focused Parent | CPA | 500 clicks each |
| Targeting | Interest-based | Broad (let Meta optimize) | CPA + ROAS | 500 clicks each |

### Week 3-4: Offer Testing

| Test | Variable A | Variable B | Success Metric | Min Sample |
|------|-----------|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| Default pricing | 1-pack ($14.99) featured | 3-pack ($34.99) featured | AOV + Revenue | 200 landing page visits each |
| Landing page | Long-form (with FAQ) | Short-form (hero + pricing + CTA) | Conversion rate | 300 visits each |
| Social proof | Testimonials | "X profiles completed" counter | Conversion rate | 300 visits each |

### Week 4+: Iteration

| Test | Variable A | Variable B | Success Metric |
|------|-----------|-----------|----------------|
| UGC vs produced | iPhone-quality testimonial | Polished brand video | CPA |
| Sport-specific ads | Generic "athlete" creative | Soccer-specific creative | CPA by sport |
| Retargeting timing | 3-day retarget | 7-day retarget | Conversion rate |
| Google ad copy | Pain point headline | Benefit headline | CTR + conversion |

### Testing Rules

1. **One variable at a time.** Never test creative and audience simultaneously.
2. **Statistical significance before killing.** Minimum 100 conversions per variant or 7 days, whichever comes first.
3. **Winner takes 70%.** When a winner is clear, allocate 70% of budget to winner, 30% to next test.
4. **Document everything.** Log results in a shared sheet: date, test, variants, impressions, clicks, conversions, CPA, winner.
5. **Creative fatigue check.** Rotate winning ads every 3-4 weeks. Frequency above 3.0 = time for new creative.

## 9. Quick-Win Opportunities

1. **Blog-to-ad pipeline:** Turn top-performing archetype blog posts into carousel ads. Content is already validated by organic traffic.

2. **Sport-specific seasonal pushes:** Time soccer ads to registration weeks (Aug, Feb). Baseball to spring training (Mar). Swimming to summer league sign-ups (May).

3. **Parent Facebook groups:** Not ads, but organic seeding. Share the lead magnet PDF in youth sports parent groups (with admin permission). Drives low-cost email capture.

4. **Referral program:** Post-purchase email includes "Share with a sports parent" link. Referred friend gets free archetype mini-guide. Referrer gets nothing tangible (parents share because the product is interesting, not for discounts).

5. **Bundle anchoring:** On the landing page, show the 5-pack first ($49.99 = $10/profile), then 3-pack ($34.99 = $11.66/profile), then 1-pack ($14.99). Anchoring makes the 3-pack feel like the smart choice.
